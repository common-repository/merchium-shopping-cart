Merchium (beta) is a powerful online store right in your WordPress blog. Get started in seconds!

== Description ==

[Merchium](http://merchium.com) (beta) is the most powerful shopping cart for your WordPress website.

Start your online store in seconds: just install the Merchium WordPress plugin and complete the short setup. In a moment, your store will be set up and ready to sell!

= Portable power =

Merchium is unbeatable in respect of feature-richness. Your customers will enjoy seamless shopping experience with fast cloud search and one-page checkout, and you as a storeowner will have the access tons of advanced e-commerce features, like flexible promotion system, built-in CMS, etc. 

Merchium supports **45+ payment gateways** right off the bat and lets you take payments with credit cards, PayPal, and other popular options.

Thanks to integration with major shipping services like **FedEx and USPS**, Merchium calculates shipping rates on the fly.

Merchium is localized into **over a dozen languages** and lets your customers change between currencies; all prices are converted on the fly.

= Easy to customize =

Merchium has an on-site **visual editor** that lets you change your storefront's color scheme, fonts, backgrounds, and more. No HTML or CSS knowledge required!

You can create a specific layout and choose a style for every location you have a storefront at, e.g. WordPress, Facebook, company's website, etc.

= Mobile-friendly =

Merchium stores look great on all screen sizes thanks to its responsive design and the specially crafted **mobile storefront**.

The mobile storefront was created from scratch with touch interface in mind. It utilizes familiar gestures like swipe to deliver the best shopping experience to mobile users.

= Facebook store =

You can embed your Merchium store right in your **Facebook** page. Your catalog and orders will be synced across all your storefronts.

Actually, you can embed a storefront in any website that allows custom JS: Joomla, Wix, WordPress, and so on.

Learn more about Merchium at [merchium.com](http://merchium.com).

== Installation ==

= Automatic =

1. Go to your **WordPress admin panel → Plugins**
1. Click **Add New**
1. Type *Merchium* in the search box and click **Search Plugins**
1. Click **Install Now** under the **Merchium Shopping Cart** entry
1. After the plugin is installed, click **Activate Plugin**
1. Go to **Merchium Store** in your WordPress admin panel and follow the setup instructions


= Manual =

1. Download the plugin from this page
1. Go to your **WordPress admin panel → Plugins**
1. Click **Add New**
1. Click **Upload**
1. Click **Browse...**, and pick the saved plugin archive (.zip file)
1. Click **Install Now**
1. After the plugin is installed, click the **Activate Plugin** link
1. Go to **Merchium Store** in your WordPress admin panel and follow the setup instructions


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Is it free? =

Yes, Merchium can be used for free, on the *Starter* plan.

= Do I need a credit card to register? =

Nope. You'll just need an e-mail to register. The registration is absolutely free. And it won't take longer than a few seconds.

= How is it better than Ecwid? =

Merchium is more feature rich. Whereas Ecwid is a very basic shopping cart, Merchium has many [advanced features](http://www.merchium.com/features-design/).
